Deep Matrix Factorization Approach for Collaborative Filtering Recommender Systems¶

By Ral Lara-Cabrera, ngel Gonzlez-Prieto, and Fernando Ortega

General idea¶

Using the MF paradigm, introduces the principles of DL to refine the output of the model through successive trainings.

Operation¶

Model¶

Output: $$ \begin{aligned} R \approx \hat{R} &=\hat{E}^{0}+E^{1}=\hat{E}^{0}+\hat{E}^{1}+E^{2}=\ldots \approx \hat{E}^{0}+\hat{E}^{1}+\hat{E}^{2}+\ldots+\hat{E}^{N} = P^{0} \cdot Q^{0}+P^{1} \cdot Q^{1}+\cdots+P^{N} \cdot Q^{N}=\sum_{s=0}^{N} P^{s} \cdot Q^{s} \end{aligned} $$

the loss function is given by

$$ \begin{aligned} \mathcal{F}^{S}\left(p_{u}^{S}, q_{i}^{S}\right) &=\left\|E^{S}-P^{s} \cdot Q^{S}\right\|^{2}+\lambda^{S}\left(\sum_{u=1}^{n}\left\|p_{u}^{S}\right\|^{2}+\sum_{i=1}^{m}\left\|q_{i}^{S}\right\|^{2}\right) =\sum_{e_{u, i}^{s} \neq \bullet}\left(e_{u, i}^{S}-p_{u}^{S} \cdot q_{i}^{S}\right)^{2}+\lambda^{S}\left(\sum_{u=1}^{n}\left\|p_{u}^{S}\right\|^{2}+\sum_{i=1}^{m}\left\|q_{i}^{S}\right\|^{2}\right) \end{aligned} $$

update rules

$$ \begin{aligned} p_{u}^{s} & \leftarrow p_{u}^{s}+\gamma^{s}\left(\left(e_{u, i}^{s}-p_{u}^{s} \cdot q_{i}^{s}\right) \cdot q_{i}^{S}-\lambda^{s} p_{u}^{s}\right) \\ q_{i}^{s} & \leftarrow q_{i}^{s}+\gamma^{s}\left(\left(e_{u, i}^{s}-p_{u}^{s} \cdot q_{i}^{s}\right) \cdot p_{u}^{s}-\lambda^{s} q_{i}^{s}\right) \end{aligned} $$

where $\gamma^{s}$ is the learning rate hyper-parameter of the s-th step to control the learning speed.
